The 6-2-1 Rule

6 hours of sleep

2 full meals

1 shower

The 6-2-1 rule is a reminder of some basic health and hygeine practices you should follow when you go to a convention. Remember that these are per day and should be considered minimums.

  • 6 hours of sleep: A good night’s sleep makes sure you’re well rested and focused.
  • 2 full meals: Proper meals give you the energy you need to enjoy the convention.
  • 1 shower: A good shower (or bath) makes sure you’re clean and smelling fresh, which is especially important when you’re in crowded spaces.
