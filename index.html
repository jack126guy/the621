The 6-2-1 Rule

6 hours of sleep

2 full meals

1 shower

The 6-2-1 rule is a reminder of some basic health and hygiene practices for conventions. Although originally from science-fiction fandom (and later popularized through the furry fandom), the recommendations apply to events of all types.

  • 6 hours of sleep: A good night’s sleep makes sure you’re well rested and focused.
  • 2 full meals: Proper meals give you the energy you need to enjoy the convention.
  • 1 shower: A good shower (or bath) makes sure you’re clean and smelling fresh, which is especially important when you’re in crowded spaces.

Important Notes

  • Each item is per day and should be considered a minimum. If you can do more, then do more!
  • These are not the only things you need to do to stay healthy at a convention.
